# Li Qingzhao

# Personal profile

Song Dynasty female writer

Li Qingzhao (March 13, 1084 - about 1155), No. Yi Anju, Han, Qizhou Jinan (now Zhangqiu District, Jinan City, Shandong Province). The female poet in the Song Dynasty, the representative of the slogan, is known as the "first talented woman of the ages."

  • 《Such as dreams, last night, rain and wind》
